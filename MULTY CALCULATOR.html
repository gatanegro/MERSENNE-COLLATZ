Perform calculations on multiple numbers and export results

Input Parameters
Add (+)
Subtract (-)
Multiply (×)

How to use:

  • Enter numbers separated by commas or spaces
  • Select the operation (add, subtract, multiply, or divide)
  • Enter the value to use in the operation
  • Click "Calculate" to see results
  • Click "Export Results" to copy results to clipboard
